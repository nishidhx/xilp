## Step 1: Understand the Tech Fields

### 1. Web Development

What it is

Building websites and web applications.

Languages & skills

- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular, Tailwind, Bootstrap
- Backend: Node.js, Python (Flask/Django)
- Databases: SQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB

Why choose it

Easy to start, high demand, and many beginner-friendly learning paths.

### 2. Mobile App Development

What it is

Creating native or cross-platform apps for Android and iOS.

Languages & skills

- Flutter (Dart) for cross-platform apps
- Java/Kotlin for Android
- Swift for iOS

Why choose it

Mobile-first products are prevalent; app development skills remain in demand.

### 3. Data Science & AI / ML

What it is

Using data to build models, make predictions, and extract insights.

Languages & skills

- Python (core language)
- Libraries: NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch
- Math: Linear algebra, statistics, probability

Why choose it

Fast-growing field with opportunities in research, product, and analytics.

### 4. Cybersecurity

What it is

Protecting systems, networks, and data from threats and vulnerabilities.

Languages & skills

- Networking fundamentals
- Linux and command-line proficiency
- Scripting: Python, Bash
- Tools: Wireshark, Burp Suite, Metasploit

Why choose it

Security is critical across industries; demand for skilled professionals is increasing.

### 5. Cloud & DevOps

What it is

Managing infrastructure, deployments, and automation for scalable systems.

Languages & skills

- Linux and shell scripting
- Cloud platforms: AWS, Azure, GCP
- Tools: Docker, Kubernetes, Git, CI/CD pipelines

Why choose it

Essential for modern production-grade applications and operations.

Do you enjoy building things visually? → Try Web or Mobile Development.

Do you love math and patterns? → Go for AI/ML or Data Science.

Are you curious about hacking and protecting systems? → Explore Cybersecurity.

Do you like infrastructure and automation? → Check out Cloud & DevOps.
